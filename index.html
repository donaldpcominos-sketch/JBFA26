How it works

Runs in parallel with the Main Season Comp across all 27 rounds. Each round the coaches with the lowest score that week are eliminated — regardless of how they've performed before. Weekly score only, never cumulative.

* Tiebreaker: if coaches finish on the same score at the cut line, the coach with the higher score from the previous round is retained. Ties cascade back further if still equal. In Round 1 (no prior round), tied coaches at the cut are both kept safe.

Understanding Trade %

% of field — The percentage of all JBFA coaches who traded this player in or out this round.

% eligible — The percentage of coaches who could have made the trade and chose to do so.

Example: If a player was owned by 10% of coaches before the round, and 9% of all coaches traded him out, then 90% of eligible coaches chose to move him on — the % eligible tells you how decisive the trade move was.

Note: A coach can only trade out a player they own, so % eligible is always ≥ % of field for trades out.
